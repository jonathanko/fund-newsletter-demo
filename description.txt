Telligent is an actively managed equity long/short fund that seeks compounded long-term returns by investing in listed securities, primarily in the United States, Taiwan, and Hong Kong, of superior businesses with competitive advantages that are within the investment mandate.

The investment approach involves utilizing fundamental research and supply chain analysis.  The investment decision-making thrives by adopting a global perspective with local insights.  The portfolio holdings are expected to substantially benefit from the big trends and paradigm shifts occurring under uncertain and volatile market conditions.
